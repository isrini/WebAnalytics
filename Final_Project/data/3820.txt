Road Hard is a 2015 comedy film directed by Adam Carolla and written by Adam Carolla and Kevin Hench. The film stars Carolla, Diane Farr, Larry Miller, David Allen Grier, and David Koechner. The film was released in selected theaters and on Video-on-Demand on March 6, 2015. This was Windell Middlebrooks' last film, as he died 3 days after the premiere.Years after his movie and sitcom career has run dry, Bruce Madsen is reduced to headlining one dingy comedy club after another, spending his nights in budget hotel rooms, and flying coach while his former fans sit in first class. He has only one question: What the hell happened? Amidst trying to revitalize his career, rekindle his love life, and put his daughter through college, Bruce knows one thing for sure - he must get off the road - hard. Road Hard is the story of that journey.
