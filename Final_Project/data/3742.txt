Pernicious is a Thai-American supernatural horror film directed by James Cullen Bressack,[1] who also wrote the story along with co-writer Taryn Hillin.[2] The film stars Ciara Hanna, Emily O'Brien, and Jackie Moore.[3]   Pernicious is produced by Benetone Hillin Entertainment, a company formed from the partnership of Daemon Hillin and Benetone Films, a leading production service company in Thailand.[4] The movie was shot completely in Thailand. Vantage Media International is reported to represent world rights to the movie.[5]
