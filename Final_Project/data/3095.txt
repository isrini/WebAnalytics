Lola Versus is a 2012 American romantic comedy film directed by Daryl Wein. The screenplay was co-written by Wein and his partner and Lola co-star Zoe Lister-Jones.[1][2][3] It stars Greta Gerwig, Joel Kinnaman, Zoe Lister Jones, Bill Pullman and Debra Winger.   Lola Versus received mixed reviews during its opening weeks, some positive and some extremely negative.[4][5][6][7] Review aggregation website Rotten Tomatoes gives the film a 35% rating based on 77 reviews.[citation needed]
