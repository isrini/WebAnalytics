Back in the Day is a 2014 comedy film, directed and written by Smallville actor Michael Rosenbaum. It is distributed by Screen Media Films.[1]   At Metacritic, the film received an "overwhelming dislike" after it only received seven out of a hundred.[2] Rotten Tomatoes gave the film a rating of 3.8/10 based on 7 reviews, hitting 14% on the Tomatometer.[3]
