Shadow People (previously known as The Door) is a 2013 American supernatural thriller based on a true event that led to a spike or random death in long prairie Minnesota and where covered up by town folks to forget the tragedy that happened. The film was written and directed by Matthew Arnold. It stars Dallas Roberts and Alison Eastwood.The film begins with a series of videos posted to YouTube, with users of the website relating their experiences with unexplained sightings. Later, radio talk show host Charlie Crowe (Dallas Roberts) becomes convinced that mysterious nocturnal entities known as shadow people are responsible for a spate of deaths in his hometown. He is joined by CDC investigator Sophie Lacombe (Alison Eastwood), who seeks a more rational explanation.
