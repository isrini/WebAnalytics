Two Weeks is a 2006 American comedy-drama film written and directed by Steve Stockman and starring Sally Field. It came out in theaters on March 2, 2007 in select theaters and was released on DVD on September 18, 2007. However, it was screened at the Hamptons International Film Festival on October 20, 2006 and is often cited as a 2006 film.Four siblings return home to their mother's house, in North Carolina, for what they think are the last few days of her life. Suddenly this unexpected family reunion extends uncomfortably as she hangs on and they find themselves trapped together for two weeks, forced to face her death and also their connection with each other.
