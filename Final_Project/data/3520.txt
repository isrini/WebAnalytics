Straight A's is a 2013 American romantic comedy film[2] directed by James Cox and produced by Jamie Adamic. It stars Anna Paquin, Ryan Phillippe and Luke Wilson in the lead role. The film was released on January 13, 2013, in Brazil, on March 19, 2013, in the USA and on June 5, 2013, in the Netherlands.
