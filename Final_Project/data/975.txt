Bryan Woods (born September 14, 1984) is an American film director and screenwriter from Davenport, Iowa. He co-writes and co-directs with filmmaking partner Scott Beck. In 2001, Beck & Woods formed Bluebox Films. Together they have written and directed Spread, a scripted pilot for MTV,[1] and Nightlight, a supernatural thriller for Lionsgate and Herrick Entertainment.[2] Beck & Woods are attached to direct XOXO, a script written by Black Swan writer Mark Heyman and produced by Darren Aronofsky.[3]
