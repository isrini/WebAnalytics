The 8th Plague is a 2006 low-budget American horror film starring DJ Perry, Leslie Ann Valenza, and Charles Edwin Powell and is directed by Franklin Guerrero Jr.[1][2]Launa investigates the disappearance of her sister Nikki who never returned from a camping trip in the fictional town of Halcyon Springs. A local, named Mason, helps by leading her to an abandoned prison. Upon entering Launa comes in contact with evil, ancient, supernatural powers.[3][4]
