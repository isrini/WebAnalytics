Once in a Lifetime: The Extraordinary Story of the New York Cosmos is a 2006 documentary film about the New York Cosmos, one of the most famous soccer clubs in the history of the United States.   The movie premiered on July 7, 2006 in the New York City area. Miramax distributed the film only in limited release.
