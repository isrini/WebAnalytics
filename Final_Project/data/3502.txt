Shotgun Wedding is an American black comedy film produced by Fox Studios. It premiered on Netflix on April 1, 2013.[1]While drunk, Robert (Mike Damus), the groom-to-be, 'accidentally' shoots the maid of honor of his fiance in the face during a skeet-shooting event, and his scheming mother does whatever needed to ensure the wedding will occur, even to conspire with her ex-husband or kidnap the maid of honor.[2]
