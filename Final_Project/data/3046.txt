The Fitzgerald Family Christmas is a 2012 comedy-drama film starring Edward Burns and Connie Britton.[2] The film, written, directed, and produced by Burns was premiered at the 2012 Toronto International Film Festival. It has been met with positive reviews from critics with 64% on Rotten Tomatoes.[3]The seven adult siblings of the Fitzgerald family prepare for their estranged father to return home for Christmas for the first time since he walked out on his family 20 years ago.[4]
