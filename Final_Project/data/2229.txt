Spy School (also known as Doubting Thomas) is a 2008 American comedy-drama film, released outside the United States as Doubting Thomas or Lies and Spies.[1] The films stars Forrest Landis and AnnaSophia Robb as the lead characters. The movie focuses on the adventures of Thomas Miller, in his efforts to save the President's daughter from being kidnapped.A twelve-year-old boy known for telling tall tales, overhears a plot to kidnap the President's daughter. When he goes public with his story, no one believes him, and he is forced to save her on his own.
