Listen Up Philip is a 2014 American comedy-drama film written and directed by Alex Ross Perry.[3][4] The film had its world premiere at 2014 Sundance Film Festival on January 20, 2014.[5][6]Philip (Jason Schwartzman) is an acclaimed but abrasive young writer waiting for the publication of his second novel. He feels bored of his daily life and his shaky relationship with photographer girlfriend Ashley (Elisabeth Moss). In all of this chaos, his idol, veteran novelist Ike Zimmerman (Jonathan Pryce), offers him accommodation at his summer home, an isolated place where he might find peace.
