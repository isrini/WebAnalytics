Justin Bieber's Believe is a 2013 American biographical concert film[4][5][6] and the sequel to Never Say Never, both centering on Canadian singer Justin Bieber. It was released through Open Road Films in the United States and Canada on December 25, 2013. The film received negative reviews from critics and grossed $6.2 million in the United States.The sequel to Never Say Never continues to focus on Bieber's rise to international fame as he embarks on his Believe Tour. In new interviews with Bieber, the movie reveals long-awaited answers to questions about his passion to make music, relationships and coming of age in the spotlight as well as never-before-seen concert footage, and behind-the-scenes access. The film also features interviews with Justin's mother Pattie Mallette, mentor Usher Raymond IV, manager Scooter Braun, recording artist Ludacris, as well as others.
