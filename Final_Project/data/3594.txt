Someone Marry Barry is a 2014 American Romantic comedy directed and written by Rob Pearlstein. The film stars Tyler Labine, Lucy Punch, Damon Wayans, Jr., and Hayes MacArthur.   On July 23, 2012, The Hollywood Reporter reported that Labine, Punch, and Wayans, Jr. had joined the cast of the film written and directed by Pearlstein.[2][3] On November 1, 2013, it was announced that FilmBuff will release the film on February 13, 2014 in theaters and on VOD.[4] The principal photography of the film began on July 23, 2012 in Los Angeles.[2][3]
