That's What I Am is a 2011 comedy-drama film directed by Michael Pavone and starring Ed Harris and Chase Ellison.[2] It received a limited release on April 29, 2011, and was later released on DVD on July 15, 2011.   In its limited theatrical release of 10 theaters, the film grossed about $6,400 for three days.[3] The film holds a 60% rating on Rotten Tomatoes.[4]
