Day Night Day Night is a 2006 drama film written and directed by Julia Loktev, starring Luisa Williams. It documents 48 hours in the life of an anonymous nineteen-year-old woman who plans to become a suicide bomber in Times Square.   It premiered at the 2006 Directors' Fortnight section of the 58th Annual Cannes Film Festival, where it won the Prix Regards Jeune.[1]
