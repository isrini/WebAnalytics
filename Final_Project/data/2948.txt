BearCity 2: The Proposal is a 2012 American gay-themed (in particular, the gay bear community) comedy-drama film written and directed by Doug Langway.[1] It is a sequel to his 2010 film BearCity.[1]Following the legalization of same-sex marriage in New York, Roger (Gerald McCullouch) proposes to his boyfriend Tyler (Joe Conti), and the gang embarks on a road trip to have the wedding in Provincetown, Massachusetts during Bear Week.
