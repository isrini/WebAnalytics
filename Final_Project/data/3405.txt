The Last of Robin Hood is a 2013 American independent biographical drama film about actor Errol Flynn which is directed and written by Richard Glatzer and Wash West.[2] The film stars Kevin Kline, Dakota Fanning, Susan Sarandon, Matthew Kane and Max Casella.
